Schedule Appointment

When you select the appointment type, you will be presented with a list of items that you must bring or do prior to your scheduled visit.

When you are done scheduling your appointment you will be prompted to print out an appointment receipt. It will contain your appointment information, including the date, time, service and address of the MVD office you selected.

Do I need to schedule an appointment?

No, but it is recommended.

Why isn't the MVD office I want to go to listed?

If the office is not listed, it's likely because they don't offer the service you requested. You will need to select one of the offices indicated on the map.

Why are there no times listed for the date I selected?

The appointments are all filled for that date. Select a different date until you find one with available time slots.

 

Appointment Confirmed

When you are done scheduling your appointment you can return to the main AZ MVD Now app by closing the current browser window or clicking on the AZ MVD Now browser tab to return to the main site.

Do I need to bring the appointment confirmation with me?

No, but it is recommended.

How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

To cancel or reschedule, return to the main AZMVDNow.gov page and select Schedule an Appointment. Any appointments you currently have scheduled will be listed. Delete your existing appointment and schedule a new appointment for a time and location that works for you.
